## Why this topic

High-intent, high-frequency pain point not yet covered by the existing dispatches. Merchants ask
constantly how to require a minimum cart value or minimum order quantity before checkout, and Shopify
has no native setting for it on standard plans. The minimum-purchase field only gates a discount, not
the checkout; Shopify Plus can block a checkout with Functions and B2B quantity rules apply to company
profiles, but neither covers the retail storefront. Distinct from every sibling: it is a checkout-floor
problem, not inventory, fulfilment, or post-order work.
