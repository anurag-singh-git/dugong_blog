  163-character `<meta name="description">`, over the ~160-char SERP limit that every other page

  "…so you lose winnable disputes"). No em/en dashes introduced; the og/twitter descriptions were
  already independent and fine, so they were left untouched. All nine meta descriptions are now in
  the 100–160 range.
