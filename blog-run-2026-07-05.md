## New post

**Dispatch Nº 76 — "The minimum order Shopify won't enforce"**
`how-to-set-a-minimum-order-amount-on-shopify.html` · by Priya Singh · 2026-07-05 · ~2,204 words

Chosen because "set a minimum order amount / minimum order quantity" is one of the most-repeated,
still-unsolved requests in Shopify merchant communities, and none of the existing dispatches covered
it. Forum and guide validation is strong: recurring Shopify Community threads ("setting a minimum
quantity amount for orders," "minimum order value and quantity rules for B2B") plus a large cluster of
third-party guides all describe the same gap. It is distinct from every sibling: it is about a checkout
floor (cart value or unit count), not inventory, fulfilment, or post-order work.

The native gap is real and specific. Shopify's checkout is built to accept any cart with at least one
item. The only "minimum" the platform understands is a minimum-purchase condition on a discount, which
gates a reward, not the door. Shopify Plus can enforce a checkout minimum with Functions (formerly
Scripts), and B2B quantity rules apply to company profiles, but neither covers the ordinary retail
storefront. The two workarounds each cost something: cart-page JavaScript that disables the checkout
button is bypassed by wallet buttons (Shop Pay, Apple Pay), saved cart links, and checkout permalinks
that never touch the cart page; and most apps enforce one flat number for everyone, unable to tell a
retail shopper from a wholesale buyer or to exempt a gift-card order. The post frames the differentiator:
not one more flat number, but detection plus judgement plus enforcement that holds on every path into
checkout, watch the cart, decide the floor for retail vs wholesale, warn the shopper early, enforce
beyond the cart page, exempt the carts that should pass, nudge the borderline cart up, and hold the real
policy calls for a human.

The H1 carries the high-intent phrasing ("How to set a minimum order amount on Shopify"). Target
phrases: "Shopify minimum order amount," "set minimum order value Shopify," "Shopify minimum order
quantity," "Shopify MOQ / wholesale minimum order," "minimum purchase before checkout Shopify,"
"Shopify minimum cart value," and "prevent small orders Shopify."
