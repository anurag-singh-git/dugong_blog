## New post

**Dispatch Nº 78 — "The invoice you send by hand"**
`how-to-automatically-send-invoices-on-shopify.html` · by Priya Singh · 2026-07-06 · ~2,290 words

Chosen because "how do I send a proper invoice / VAT invoice automatically" is one of the most-repeated,
still-unsolved requests in Shopify merchant communities, and none of the existing dispatches covered it.
Forum and guide validation is strong: recurring Shopify Community threads ("Generate VAT Receipt,"
"enabling TAX/VAT number collection in checkout," "Australian tax invoices, why is the ABN not
appearing") plus a large cluster of third-party guides and apps (Order Printer Pro, Sufio, Invoicify,
Quaderno) all describe the same gap. It is distinct from every sibling: it is about the accounting
document a buyer needs, not fulfilment, inventory, or merchandising.

The native gap is real and specific, and the post credits the parts Shopify does. Shopify auto-sends an
order confirmation email, which is a receipt, not an invoice, and carries no tax numbers. Shopify Tax
can generate a VAT invoice for orders shipped within the EU and UK and show it on the order status page,
which is a genuine compliant document, but it is never emailed to the customer, is region-locked to the
EU/UK, offers almost no customization, and cannot be regenerated after an order is edited. Most
fundamental of all, Shopify's standard checkout has no field for the customer's VAT or tax registration
number (only a company name in the billing address), so the one detail a B2B invoice legally needs is the
one the platform never captures at the source. The post frames the differentiator: not one more PDF
template, but classification + data capture + a compliant document + a reversible credit note: read the
order and whether it is consumer or business, decide receipt vs full tax invoice, fill the tax number
from notes/past orders/customer record (ask once if missing), generate a compliant sequentially numbered
PDF, send it automatically after payment, cut a matching credit note on refunds, and hold failed VAT
checks, reverse-charge/export orders, and reissue-after-edit for a human.

The heading screams the use case ("the invoice you send by hand"); the H1 carries the high-intent search
phrasing ("How to automatically send invoices to Shopify customers"). Target phrases: "Shopify send
invoice automatically," "Shopify automatic invoice," "Shopify PDF invoice," "Shopify VAT invoice," "send
invoice to customer Shopify," "Shopify B2B invoice tax number," "Shopify order invoice email," "Shopify
tax invoice," and "Shopify credit note refund."
