## Defect: incomplete back-link graph (fixed)

The daily blog-run that shipped the review-request post added inbound "Read next" cards from
only the two most related siblings (repeat-customer and wismo) and left back-links from every
older post as a deferred pass. That left the new post under-discoverable.

| Post | In-degree before | In-degree after |
|---|---|---|
| the-review-request-you-never-send.html (Nº 65) | 2 | 18 |

Every other post already sat at the full in-degree of 18. The new post itself already linked
out to all 18 siblings, and was already present in index.html, feed.xml, sitemap.xml, and
llms.txt, so only the inbound cards were missing.

The fix prepended one "Read next" card for the review-request post to the related-grid of each
of the 16 posts that lacked it (newest-first position, matching site convention). The card uses
the post's canonical metadata: tag `research`, 9 min read, title "The review request you never
send", the canonical meta-description dek, date JUN 22, author P. Singh. No dashes introduced.

After the fix: every post links exactly 18 unique siblings and has in-degree 18 — a complete
graph with no self-cards and no duplicate cards in any grid.
