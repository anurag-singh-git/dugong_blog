## Defect found and fixed

Four post files were missing the `</head>` closing tag (the head section ran `</style>` straight
into `<body>`). Browsers auto-recover, but it is invalid HTML and inconsistent with the other 34
posts. Inserted `</head>` after the closing `</style>` in:

- `how-to-add-a-gift-message-and-gift-wrap-at-checkout-on-shopify.html` (today's Nº 85)
- `how-to-automatically-pause-ads-for-out-of-stock-products-on-shopify.html`
- `how-to-set-up-tiered-volume-discounts-on-shopify.html`
- `how-to-sync-bundle-inventory-with-component-stock-on-shopify.html`

The pattern tracks the three most recent post templates plus today's post, so the template drift
began with the Nº 82–84 era; today's fix brings all 38 posts (and index) to a consistent, valid
head structure. Div/anchor balance re-verified on all four after the edit.
