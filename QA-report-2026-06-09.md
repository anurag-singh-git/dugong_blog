## Bugs found & fixed

**Two meta descriptions exceeded the SERP length limit.** Google truncates `meta name="description"`
at roughly 155 to 160 characters, so anything longer gets cut mid-sentence in results.

- `the-return-that-wont-approve-itself.html` was **202 chars**. Rewrote to **156**:
  "Shopify makes you approve every return by hand and won't run a true exchange, so refunds leak
  revenue you already earned. The AI playbook that automates it."
- `the-order-change-that-races-your-warehouse.html` was **162 chars**. Rewrote to **148**:
  "A customer emails to change their Shopify order, and the edit locks the moment you fulfil. The
  AI playbook that resolves it before the label prints."

Meaning preserved in both, and no em/en dashes were introduced (matches the house style). The longer
`og:description` and `twitter:description` on these pages were left as-is; those are not subject to
the 160-char SERP limit. After the fix, all eight `meta name="description"` values are 100 to 159 chars.

## Verified — no issues

- **Investigated a suspected phantom post.** The homepage JSON-LD `Blog` node lists 8 `blogPost`
  entries against only 7 post files. Confirmed legitimate: entry #8 is the inline lead essay
  ("The death of the drag-and-drop builder", Nº 47) that lives on the index page itself at the
  `#post` route, not a missing file. The other 7 map 1:1 to the live post files.
- **Investigated a suspected broken `#post` anchor.** There is no `id="post"` element, but `#post`
  is never used as a scroll anchor: the site is a JS-routed SPA and the `data-nav="post"` handler
  calls `preventDefault()` and swaps to `id="view-post"`. The `#post` href is only a no-JS fallback.
- **Card navigation.** All 19 real `*.html` links on the homepage sit outside any `data-nav`
  container, so they navigate to the correct post files. The decorative teaser cards / archive rows
  (e.g. "Seven prompts…", archive Nº 041–036) intentionally route to the in-page lead essay; none
  of them masquerade as a missing file.
- **Internal links:** every local href across all 8 pages resolves (0 broken). No post's
  "Read next" block links to itself.
- **Feed / sitemap / llms.txt:** all parse; each lists exactly the same 7 post URLs (feed and
  sitemap carry 8 entries = 7 posts + homepage). Feed `lastBuildDate` Tue, 09 Jun 2026; all
  sitemap `lastmod` 2026-06-09.
- **Metadata:** canonical matches `og:url` on every page; titles present and unique; `dateModified`
  2026-06-09 on all posts; JSON-LD parses on all 8 files.
- **HTML structure:** `<div>` open/close balanced on every file.
