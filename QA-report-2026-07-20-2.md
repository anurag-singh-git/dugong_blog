## Issue found and fixed: mixed British/American spellings

Six posts mixed "fulfilment" with "fulfillment", several within the same article (edit-order had 12
British vs 3 American; validate-addresses 10 vs 4). Bare "fulfil" also appeared across most files,
and "colour" appeared 8 times in the returns post (which uses "color" elsewhere in prose) plus once
in llms.txt. Site-wide the American forms dominate (22 files use "fulfillment"; "fulfills" and
"fulfilled" are already the double-l American forms everywhere), and Shopify's own product naming is
"Fulfillment", so everything was normalized to American spelling:

| Replacement | Occurrences fixed |
|---|---|
| fulfilment → fulfillment | 35 |
| fulfil → fulfill (word-boundary safe) | 90 |
| colour → color | 10 |
