  is an editorial change, out of scope for an automated QA fix.

- HowTo / step ItemList schema for older posts' playbook sequences remains an open GEO
  opportunity flagged in prior SEO passes, not a correctness defect.
- dateModified across older posts left untouched — adding a navigation card is not a content
  edit and a site-wide dateModified bump would send a misleading freshness signal.
