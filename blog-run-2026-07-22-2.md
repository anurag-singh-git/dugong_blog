"I need to send a custom message to customers about a shipping delay," "How can I notify
customers about shipping delays during vacation?", and the precise engineering ask, "Shopify Flow
auto email to notify customer of delay for unfulfilled orders older than 7 days." Third-party
coverage is thin (a couple of notification app vendors plus community reposts), while the demand
is old and steady, which fits the brief of high demand and low existing content. No existing
dispatch covered it: the closest posts (WISMO tickets, tracking numbers, preorders) sit upstream
or downstream of the gap but none addresses the delay notice itself.

The native gap is real, specific, and credited: Shopify's transactional emails fire reliably at
their two moments (order confirmation at checkout, shipping confirmation when a fulfillment is
created), and between them there is nothing. The walls: (1) no delay notice template and no
trigger for an order aging past its promised ship time; (2) the Orders page filters to open
unfulfilled orders but has no bulk email behind the filter, so the native paths are one order at
a time or CSV export into an external mail tool; (3) Shopify Flow can find the late orders (a
scheduled trigger can query paid orders unfulfilled after 7 days; the Wait action holds up to 30
days) but its only native email action is Send internal email, which writes to staff, never the
customer; (4) Shopify Email campaigns reach only marketing subscribers, and a delay notice is
transactional, so guest buyers are unreachable. All facts verified against Shopify help docs
(Wait action, Send internal email) and community threads. Why it matters: WISMO tickets, item-
not-received chargebacks, cancellations timed at the worst moment, and reviews that name the
silence rather than the product. The judgement framing: specificity is the apology, the generic
blast is the trap, and a second broken promise costs more than the first.

The pain-point heading screams the use case ("the delayed order no one tells your customer
about"); the H1 carries the high-intent search phrasing ("How to notify customers of shipping
delays on Shopify automatically"). Target phrases: "Shopify notify customers of shipping delay,"
"Shopify shipping delay email," "Shopify delayed order notification," "Shopify bulk email
unfulfilled orders," "Shopify Flow email customer unfulfilled order," "Shopify fulfillment delay
notification," "Shopify email customers with open orders," "proactive shipping delay
communication."
