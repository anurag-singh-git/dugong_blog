## Factual grounding

Claims verified against Shopify Community threads (multiple long-running merge/combine requests) and
current 2026 write-ups on combining orders. Key facts asserted: Shopify has no native order-merge
feature and Shopify Flow cannot merge orders; each order is a fixed financial record (payment, tax,
discount, fulfillment history), which is the stated reason merging is not native; the accepted
workaround is a manual draft-order routine (rebuild line items into one draft, single label, add
tracking to originals, refund duplicate shipping, cancel originals and restock); third-party merge apps
(e.g. Mergify, Order Merging) exist, which corroborates the native gap.
