**Dispatch Nº 75 — "The sold-out variant Shopify still shows"**
`how-to-automatically-hide-sold-out-variants-on-shopify.html` · by Priya Singh · 2026-07-03 · ~2,108 words

Chosen because "hide sold-out / out-of-stock variants" is one of the most-repeated, still-unsolved
requests in Shopify merchant communities, and none of the existing 27 dispatches covered it. It is
distinct from the two nearest siblings: the collection-sorting post (push sold-out products to the
bottom) is about collection order, and the back-in-stock post is about the waitlist; neither is about
hiding an individual variant on the product page. Forum validation is strong: recurring Shopify
Community threads ("Hide specific variants when sold out," "Hidding out of stock product variants") and
a large cluster of third-party guides all describe the same pain: when a size or color sells out,
Shopify keeps the variant in the dropdown/swatch shown as Sold Out or Unavailable, and there is no
native setting to hide it.

The native gap is real and specific. Shopify treats the variant list as product structure, not a live
view of what is buyable, so the theme renders every option value regardless of stock. Turning off
"continue selling when out of stock" only disables purchase, the variant still shows; the newer
"unlisted" status hides a whole product, not a variant. The only real routes are hand-editing the
theme's Liquid (brittle, theme-bound, breaks on updates, and much harder once size and color combine,
because a color is only truly gone when every size in it is out) or a third-party app that hides on a
fixed everything-at-zero rule. The post frames the differentiator: not "one more toggle" but detection
plus judgement plus a reversible action that runs both ways: watch inventory per variant, decide hide
vs keep-with-a-signup, hide cleanly without unpublishing, collapse an option only when all its values
are gone, capture the hidden demand, restore the moment stock returns, and hold merchandising calls for
a human.

The heading screams the use case for anyone living it, and the H1 carries the high-intent search
phrasing ("How to automatically hide sold-out variants on Shopify"). Target phrases: "Shopify hide sold
out variants," "hide out of stock variants Shopify," "hide unavailable variants Shopify," "Shopify
variant dropdown sold out," "remove sold out sizes Shopify," and "hide out of stock variant
automatically Shopify."
