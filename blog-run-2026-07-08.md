## New post

**Dispatch Nº 80 — "The free gift that never lands in the cart"**
`how-to-automatically-add-a-free-gift-with-purchase-on-shopify.html` · by Priya Singh · 2026-07-08 · ~2,460 words

Chosen because "how do I automatically add a free gift to the cart," "free gift when a customer
spends over an amount," and "why does my Buy X Get Y not show the gift in the cart" are among the
most-repeated, still-unsolved promotion questions for any store running a gift-with-purchase offer,
and no existing dispatch covered it. Forum and app-ecosystem validation is strong: the Shopify
community threads spell out the native gaps almost verbatim, and a dense cluster of gift/BOGO apps
(EG Auto Add to Cart, BOGOS, Gift Box, Corner, and others) exists precisely because the native
platform cannot add the item.

The native gap is real and specific, and the post credits what Shopify does. Buy X Get Y genuinely
handles a fixed, product-specific bundle and prices the cheapest qualifying item to zero. But (1) a
discount only reduces a price, it never adds the "get" item, so if the gift is not already in the
cart nothing appears; (2) there is no store-wide cart-value trigger, so "spend $100 on anything, get
a gift" is not natively expressible (Buy X Get Y fires on specific products/collections only); (3)
the native rule supports one tier per discount and will not stack, so a larger automatic discount can
silently swallow the gift. The common cart-page-script workaround leaks because wallet buttons
(Shop Pay/Apple Pay/Google Pay), buy-it-now, saved cart links, and checkout permalinks never touch
the cart page, and add-only code leaves the gift on a cart that fell back under the threshold. Shopify
Flow reacts after the order is placed, too late to touch the live cart. The post frames the
differentiator: not one more gift rules-table, but a read of the live cart + a decision by
threshold/tier/segment/exclusions + an action on every checkout path + corrections as the cart moves
(swap/remove on drop, upgrade on higher tier), with judgement (near-empty gift stock, gamed carts,
kept-gift returns, risky stacks) escalated to a human.

The heading screams the use case ("the free gift that never lands in the cart"); the H1 carries the
high-intent search phrasing ("How to automatically add a free gift to the cart on Shopify"). Target
phrases: "Shopify free gift with purchase," "Shopify auto add free gift to cart," "Shopify buy x get
y not adding to cart," "Shopify gift with purchase threshold," "Shopify free gift over amount,"
"Shopify BOGO auto add," "Shopify free gift app," "Shopify cart value gift," and "Shopify automatic
gift."
